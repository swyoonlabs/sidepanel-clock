🕐 Side Panel Clock — Clock, World Clock, Timer & Pomodoro, always by your side

Keep a clean, glanceable clock right in your browser sidebar — no extra tab, no switching away from what you're doing.

Side Panel Clock lives in Chrome's Side Panel, so the time is always one glance away while you work, read, or study.

✨ Key Features:
• 🕐 Always-on Clock — A large digital + analog clock stays pinned to the top of the panel so you can check the time at a glance. Switch 12/24-hour, seconds, and analog on or off.
• 🌍 World Clock — Track major cities around the world with their local time, date, and offset from you. Perfect for remote teams, family abroad, or scheduling across time zones. Add and remove cities freely.
• ⏲️ Timer — A clean countdown with a circular progress ring. Use quick presets (1 / 3 / 5 / 10 / 25 min) or set your own hours, minutes, and seconds. Get a sound and a notification when time's up — even if the panel is closed.
• 🍅 Pomodoro — Built-in focus timer that cycles focus → short break → long break automatically, with a progress ring and a daily session count. Customize focus and break lengths to fit how you work.
• 🌐 8 Languages — English, 한국어, 日本語, 简体中文, Español, Français, Deutsch, and Português (BR). The interface, dates, times, and city names adapt to your browser language automatically.
• 🎨 Light & Dark Themes — One tap to switch. Comfortable day or night.
• 🖥️ Feels Native — Uses your operating system's own font, so it blends right into the browser.
• 🔒 100% Private — Everything stays on your device. No accounts, no tracking, no servers. Works fully offline.

🎯 Perfect for:
• Remote workers and teams coordinating across time zones
• Anyone using the Pomodoro technique to stay focused
• People with family, friends, or clients abroad
• Anyone who just wants a good-looking clock always in view while browsing

💡 How to use:

Click the Side Panel Clock icon to open it in your sidebar.
The clock stays at the top — switch the bottom section between World Clock, Timer, and Pomodoro.
Add your cities, start a timer, or run a focus session — the panel stays open beside every tab.

Install Side Panel Clock and keep time right beside you.
