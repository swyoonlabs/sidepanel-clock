Keep time right beside you

Side Panel Clock lives right in your browser's Side Panel, so the time is always one glance away — no extra tab, no switching away from what you're doing. In one line, this first release is about "one glance for the time, the world, and your focus."

🕐 At a glance

  • 🕐 Always-on clock — a large digital + analog clock pinned to the top of the panel
  • 🌍 World clock — 20 major cities with local time, date, and offset from you
  • ⏲️ Timer — circular progress ring, quick presets, sound + notification when done
  • 🍅 Pomodoro — automatic focus/break cycles with a daily session count
  • 🌐 8 languages — localizes automatically to your browser

🕐 An always-on clock — the heart of the app

The whole point of the Side Panel is that you can keep glancing at it while doing something else. So the clock stays pinned to the top of the panel — no tab switching, no new window. While you browse, the time is always a glance away.

  • Digital + analog — turn the analog face on or off to taste.
  • 12/24-hour and seconds — toggle with a single tap.
  • Date, weekday, and AM/PM format automatically to your browser language.

🌍 World clock — no more time-zone math

Remote teammates, family abroad, meetings across time zones — you don't have to calculate the offset every time.

  • Add any of 20 major cities to see its local time, date, and offset from you.
  • Remove them anytime. City names show in your current language (Seoul / 서울 / ソウル / 首尔 …).

⏲️ Timer — see the time left in a ring

  • A circular progress ring shrinks as the countdown runs, so the time left is instantly clear.
  • Use presets (1 · 3 · 5 · 10 · 25 min) or enter your own hours, minutes, and seconds.
  • Get a sound + desktop notification when it ends — even if the panel is closed.

🍅 Pomodoro — focus and rest, automatically

25 minutes of focus, 5 minutes of rest — the proven time-management technique, built right in.

  • Cycles focus → short break → … → a long break every 4th session, automatically.
  • Counts the focus sessions you complete today (🍅), reset at midnight.
  • Set your own focus, short-break, and long-break lengths.
  • Keeps cycling in the background and notifies you on each phase change — even with the panel closed.
💡 Tip: The clock stays on top no matter which tab you switch to below — World, Timer, or Pomodoro. Pin the panel open and it rides along with every page.

🌐 Available in 8 languages

English, 한국어, 日本語, 简体中文, Español, Français, Deutsch, and Português (BR). The interface — plus dates, times, and city names — adapts to your browser language automatically.

📢 This is for you if…

  • ✅ You check the time often while browsing → an always-on clock
  • ✅ You work across time zones → the world clock
  • ✅ You need to focus → the timer and Pomodoro
