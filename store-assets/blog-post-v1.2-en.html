UPDATE · v1.2

What's new in Side Panel Clock

Since the first release, Side Panel Clock has grown a lot — while staying the same simple, always-in-view clock in your browser sidebar. Here's a roundup of what's new, with the latest v1.2 adding a meeting planner and sharper Pomodoro controls.

✨ What's new

  • 🗓️ Meeting planner — one slider, every city's time at once (v1.2)
  • 🍅 Sharper Pomodoro — see the next long break, and set its interval (v1.2)
  • 🎨 4 clock faces & 8 accent colors — make it yours
  • 🌗 Auto light/dark — follows the time of day
  • 🌍 World clock upgrades — country flags & a day/night indicator

🗓️ Meeting planner — no more time-zone math

The world clock now has a time slider. Drag it to any time and every city updates at once, so you can instantly see "if it's 3 PM here, what time is it there?" Tap Now to snap back to live. Perfect for booking calls across time zones.

  • Each city shows a country flag and a day/night indicator (dawn / day / dusk / night).
  • Offsets are exact — including half-hour and 45-minute zones — with Daylight Saving handled automatically.

🎨 Make it yours — faces, colors, and auto dark

Pick from four analog clock faces — Classic, Minimal, Roman numerals, and plain Numbers — and eight accent colors (Indigo, Ocean, Teal, Emerald, Violet, Rose, Amber, Slate) that recolor the whole panel together. Prefer it to follow your day? The new Auto theme switches between light and dark based on the time.

🍅 Pomodoro, clearer than ever

  • A "long break in N" hint shows how many focus sessions remain until the next long break.
  • The long-break interval (every N sessions) is now adjustable.
  • Skip now follows the real cycle, so it reaches the long break too.
🔒 Still 100% private. Everything stays on your device — no accounts, no tracking, no servers — and it works fully offline, in 8 languages.
